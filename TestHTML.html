Basic info about owls .

Owls are a types of birds that are nocturn (sleep by day, hunt at night) and mostly feed on mice, snakes and others of that sort. Owls come in many sizes ranging from the size of your hand to the size of your body! They live in trees, but some live nests. Theyre all fluffy feathered and soooooo cute! They each have the same adorable oval eyes and little beaks!
